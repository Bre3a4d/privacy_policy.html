Privacy Policy for Warm Reminder Popup Remover
Your privacy is our top priority. This document outlines how the Warm Reminder Popup
Remover extension operates and how it handles your data.
What This Extension Does
This extension is designed to:
Remove intrusive modal popups from specific shopping websites, including
CNFans, HooBuy, and MuleBuy.
Enable interaction with checkboxes that are disabled by default on these sites.
Clean and adjust URLs to improve navigation and add referral parameters when
necessary.
What This Extension Does NOT Do
Data Collection: This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data
from users.
External Communication: All operations performed by the extension occur locally
within your browser. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
Tracking: The extension does not track your browsing behavior, website visits, or
any other online activity.
Permissions
The following permissions are requested solely to ensure the extension functions as
intended:
activeTab: Allows the extension to modify the content of the active browser tab to
remove popups and fix checkboxes.
scripting: Enables the extension to execute scripts on specific websites to enhance
the user experience.
host permissions: Limits the extension's activity to specific websites, such as
CNFans, HooBuy, and MuleBuy.
Transparency and Security
We are committed to transparency. The extensions source code is open for review,
ensuring that users can verify its behavior. We do not sell, transfer, or misuse user data in
any form.
Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to
contact us. Your feedback and trust are highly valued.
By using the Warm Reminder Popup Remover extension, you acknowledge and agree to
this Privacy Policy.
